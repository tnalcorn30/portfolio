A bit about me:

I'm a web developer in training who is currently enrolled in the Web Development course at Eleven Fifty Academy. I've always had an interest in technology, but no clear idea of where I could potentially fit in within the tech community. I enrolled at Eleven Fifty Academy because I've been toying with the idea of learning web development for a few years now, but never really knew where to start. My coursework at Eleven Fifty is centered on learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript in order to make responsive, professional websites.

I was born and raised in a small town in southern Indiana, and after earning an AAS degree from Ivy Tech I went to work for a small business in the aforementioned small town. My life these days is an experiment in self-improvement. I'm seeking change, new experiences, and growth opportunities big and small. I'm excited to see where this new path leads me, and determined to become more than I have been. Check out my work down below, and feel free to contact me if you see something you like.
